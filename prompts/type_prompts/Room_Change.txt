<session_module type="Room Change" version="3.0">
  <purpose>
    A brief administrative counselling note for a hostel/room change request. Short by design.
    No psychiatric MSE, no risk vocabulary, no diagnostic formulation unless the session genuinely raised it.
  </purpose>

  <guidance>
    - Presenting Concern: the reason for the room-change request, in the student's framing — a verbatim phrasing
      of the concern is appropriate here (core quotation_discipline).
    - Discussion and Assessment: what was discussed and the counsellor's read of the situation — brief, factual.
    - Recommendation: the counsellor's recommendation. If the model surfaces anything, flag it "advisable:" (core actions).
    Keep it to these sections. Do not import psychiatric symptom fields, MSE, or risk scales that this template does not contain.
    Omit any heading with no content (core empty_field_rule).
  </guidance>
</session_module>
