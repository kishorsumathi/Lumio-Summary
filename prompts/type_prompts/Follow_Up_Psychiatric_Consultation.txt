<session_module type="Follow Up Psychiatric Consultation" version="3.0">
  <purpose>
    A medication-and-progress review of a known patient since the last visit. Centre of gravity:
    what changed since last contact, how medication is working, current target-symptom status, and the plan.
    This is not an intake — do not re-document full history; capture only what was reviewed this session.
  </purpose>

  <header_fields>
    The header fields (Date, Session Number, Mode of Consultation, Clinician, Patient Name, Age / Gender)
    are pre-filled from session metadata injected into the transcript block.
    Include ONLY the fields that have an actual value in the metadata.
    If Age / Gender is absent from the metadata, do NOT write "Age / Gender: Not specified" or any
    placeholder — omit the line entirely (core empty_field_rule).
  </header_fields>

  <clinical_trajectory>
    The opening line uses §FU.status below. Judge DIRECTION first (see core registrar_obligations):
    partial improvement in panic with residual anxiety is "Improving", not "Fluctuating".
    Use "Fluctuating" only when there is genuinely no net direction since last contact.
  </clinical_trajectory>

  <section name="1. Current Clinical Status">
    - Overall Status Since Last Visit: pick ONE value from §FU.status and add the single driving factor. No quote needed.
    - Patient's Subjective Report: concise paraphrase of what the patient reports this session. Paraphrase, do not quote routine narrative (travel, how a dose felt). At most one short quote, and only if the words themselves carry weight.
    - Clinician's Impression: the ONE place for formulation — mechanism + real treatment problem, ~60 words (see core formulation). Do not smear formulation into other fields.
  </section>

  <section name="2. Medication Review">
    - Current Medications table: one row per drug actually named in the session. ALWAYS name + dose together (core medication_naming). If a cell value (e.g. Dose, Frequency) is genuinely not stated in the transcript, leave that cell BLANK — never write "Not specified", "Unknown", or any placeholder text (core empty_field_rule).
    - Medication Adherence: §FU.adherence. Missed doses / Reason for Non-Adherence: only if discussed.
    - Side Effects Reported: only genuine side effects of medication. A patient's worry that an unrelated event (e.g. a vaccine) affected them is NOT a side effect — that belongs in Stressors/Triggers or is noted as a misattribution the clinician addressed. Do not place it here.
    - Response to Medications: §FU.response. Paraphrase the patient's sense of benefit; do not quote it.
  </section>

  <section name="3. Review of Target Symptoms">
    EACH target-symptom field needs CURRENT-SESSION evidence ABOUT that symptom. If there is no
    current evidence for a field, OMIT THE FIELD ENTIRELY — never include it with a disclaimer
    that the content is past or not current (see core empty_field_rule, OMIT — DO NOT ANNOTATE).

    - Mood: patient-stated mood only (§FU/MSE.mood values). Never document affect (visual).
    - Anxiety Symptoms: current anxiety presentation only.
    - Sleep (§FU.sleep), Appetite (§FU.appetite), Energy (§FU.energy): only from a direct statement about that domain now. Taking a night dose during a panic episode is adherence context, NOT sleep evidence. Weakness during a past febrile episode is NOT current baseline energy.
    - Concentration / Attention: current state only. A patient describing how poor she was at initial presentation is past content — OMIT THE FIELD; do not include the past content with a "reported as past" disclaimer.
    - Motivation / Functioning: current state only. An urge to drop out that occurred during a now-resolved past episode is a past event (→ Stressors) — OMIT THIS FIELD; do not include the past content with "not a current concern" disclaimer.
    - Irritability / Anger: only true irritability/anger content. Rejection-sensitivity, "gives more than she receives", difficulty forgiving are INTERPERSONAL themes → put them in §4 Interpersonal Relationships, not here. Omit this field if there is no anger/irritability content.
  </section>

  <section name="4. Day-to-Day Functioning">
    Report functioning AS ACTUALLY DISCUSSED in this session. Do NOT import the clinician's
    trait/personality formulation here (core formulation_containment).

    - Academic / Occupational: current functioning only. Upcoming convocation/travel is a FUTURE plan → Plan section, not current academic functioning.
    - Social Functioning: who is supporting the patient now, with whom, what was actually discussed.
    - Interpersonal Relationships: ONLY what was discussed in THIS session about relationships — e.g. what the patient herself raised, what the clinician addressed in session. Do NOT begin with "Clinician noted egosyntonic traits..." or list trait observations ("tends to feel she gives more than she receives", "difficulty forgiving", "consistent with OCPD") — that is FORMULATION and belongs only in §1 Clinician's Impression. If the only interpersonal content available is the formulation, OMIT this field.
    - ADLs / Self-Care, Routine / Structure: only if explicitly discussed.
  </section>

  <section name="5. Current Stressors / Triggers">
    Primary home for discrete stressful events (a vaccination-related episode lives HERE, once — see core one_fact_one_place).
    Recent Stressful Events / Identified Triggers / Family or Relationship Issues / Environmental Stressors — only as discussed.
  </section>

  <section name="6. Substance Use Review">
    Change Since Last Session: only if substance use was actually reviewed. Otherwise omit the section.
  </section>

  <section name="7. Mental Status Examination (Brief)">
    Audio-only — OMIT all visual MSE fields entirely (core visual_cues):
    Appearance, Behaviour, Eye contact, Psychomotor activity, AFFECT (quality/range/intensity/reactivity/congruence).
    "Grief-like affect" / "anxious affect" / "tearful" as an observation are FORBIDDEN — omit.

    Fields you MAY document if positive findings exist:
      Mood (patient-stated) / Speech (rate/tone/volume) / Thought Process / Thought Content / Perception / Insight / Judgment

    - PLACEMENT: reassurance-seeking, repeated/perseverative questioning, circumstantiality, magical thinking → Thought Process. Their CONTENT (anticipatory fears, guilt, rejection-sensitive ideation, presence/absence of suicidal ideation) → Thought Content. They are NOT Speech findings. Speech covers rate/tone/volume/quantity only.
    - Insight: include ONLY if the clinician explicitly stated an insight grade OR a discrete insight observation IN THIS SESSION. Forbidden patterns (if you find yourself writing these, OMIT the Insight field):
        · "Clinician noted patient has difficulty trusting clinical guidance..."
        · "Consistent with OCD ego-structure / psychology / structure..."
        · "Patient demonstrated some awareness of her patterns..."
        · Any range like "Grade 4–5"
      These are formulation, not insight assessment. Use an exact §MSE.insight value or OMIT.
    - Judgment: include ONLY if explicitly assessed in this session. Do not infer it from behaviour or coping choices.
  </section>

  <section name="8. Interventions / Strategies Discussed">
    One brief bullet per intervention — what was covered, not a re-teaching of it (core conciseness).
    e.g. "Psychoeducation — OCD: obsession–compulsion loop and chronic/fluctuating course covered." Not a paragraph explaining the mechanism.
  </section>

  <section name="9. Plan">
    - Medication Changes: §FU.med_change; name + dose for any change.
    - Homework / Action Steps: owner–action–trigger table (core actions).
    - Risk Level: clinician-assigned only; add the Watch / Escalate forward line (core risk_forward_line).
    - Follow-Up / Next Appointment: as stated.
  </section>

  <controlled_vocabularies>
    Reference only — never output these lists. Where a field maps to a list, use a value verbatim or omit the field.
    §FU.status (Overall status): Improving, Maintaining, Deteriorating, Fluctuating
    §FU.adherence: Fully compliant, Partially compliant, Non-compliant
    §FU.nonadherence_reason: Forgetfulness, Side effects, Financial issues, Fear of dependency, Lack of perceived benefit, Other
    §FU.response: Good response, Partial response, Minimal response, Worsening symptoms, Unable to assess
    §FU.sleep: Restful, Disturbed, Insomnia, Hypersomnia, Nightmares / Dreams
    §FU.appetite: Normal, Reduced, Increased
    §FU.energy: Adequate, Low, Fatigued, Restless
    §FU.med_change: No changes, Dose increased, Dose reduced, Medication added, Medication discontinued
    §FU.risk_level: Low, Moderate, High

    §MSE.mood (patient-stated only): Euthymic, Sad, Depressed/Low, Anxious, Fearful, Irritable, Angry, Dysphoric, Empty/Numb, Hopeless, Anhedonic, Guilty, Worthless, Lonely, Ashamed, Overwhelmed
    (Affect vocabulary intentionally omitted — affect is visual, not documentable from audio.)
    §MSE.thought_flow: Goal-directed, Circumstantiality, Tangentiality, Perseveration, Flight of ideas, Thought blocking, Magical thinking, Concrete thinking
    §MSE.thought_content: No abnormality, Obsessions, Compulsions, Ruminations, Phobias, Suicidal ideation, Self-harm ideation, Passive death-related ideation, Guilt/Worthlessness, Paranoid ideation, Ideas of reference
    §MSE.perception: No perceptual disturbance, Hallucinations, Illusions, Depersonalisation, Derealisation
    §MSE.insight (six-grade): Grade 1 complete denial · Grade 2 slight awareness, denies help needed · Grade 3 aware but blames external · Grade 4 aware it is mental, unknown cause · Grade 5 full intellectual insight · Grade 6 full emotional insight
    §MSE.judgment: Intact, Mildly impaired, Moderately impaired, Severely impaired, Not tested

    §risk.type: Passive suicidal ideation, Active ideation with plan, Self-harm/NSSI, Harm to others, Medical risk, Neglect, None identified
    §risk.ideation_plan_intent: Ideation (Present/Absent/Passive/Active) · Plan (Present/Absent/Vague/Specific) · Intent (Present/Absent/Ambivalent) · Means (Yes/No/Unknown) · Hopelessness (Present/Absent/Not assessed)
    Abuse note: any disclosure of abuse — historical or current — is documented verbatim, never softened or reclassified.
  </controlled_vocabularies>
</session_module>
